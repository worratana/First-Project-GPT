Egg Fried Rice

Egg fried rice is a classic Asian dish that features fluffy, cooked rice stir-fried with scrambled eggs, a savory mix of vegetables like peas and carrots, and often a hint of soy sauce or other seasonings. It's a quick, comforting, and versatile meal, perfect on its own or as a side.

Marmalade Sandwich

A marmalade sandwich is a classic British treat, typically made with two slices of buttered bread and a generous spread of citrus marmalade, often orange. It's known for its sweet yet tangy flavor and is famously associated with Paddington Bear, who always carries one for emergencies.

Tom Yum Soup

Tom Yum is Thailand's famous hot and sour soup, known for its vibrant and aromatic flavors. This iconic dish typically features a delightful balance of spicy, sour, and savory tastes, infused with fragrant herbs like lemongrass, galangal, and kefir lime leaves. Often made with shrimp (Tom Yum Goong), it's a refreshing and invigorating soup enjoyed across Thailand and worldwide.
