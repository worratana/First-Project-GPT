iOS 26

iOS 26 is the latest major release of Apple's operating system for iPhones, unveiled at WWDC 2025. It marks a significant shift in Apple's naming convention, now reflecting the fiscal year (2026) rather than sequential numbering (it's the successor to iOS 18, skipping what would have been iOS 19).

macOS 26

macOS 26, code-named "Tahoe," is the latest iteration of Apple's desktop operating system, announced at WWDC 2025. Similar to iOS 26, it adopts the new year-based naming convention, reflecting the 2025-2026 release cycle. This release brings a significant visual overhaul, tighter integration with other Apple devices, and enhanced productivity features, especially through a revamped Spotlight and Apple Intelligence.

iPadOS 26

iPadOS 26, unveiled at WWDC 2025, is being hailed as the "biggest iPad update ever" by Apple, transforming the tablet experience with a renewed focus on Mac-like multitasking, a stunning new design, and the integration of Apple Intelligence. It follows the new year-based naming convention, succeeding iPadOS 18 and aligning with iOS 26 and macOS 26.
