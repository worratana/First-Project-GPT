Beef Wellington Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Beef tenderloin
  • Mushrooms (e.g., wild mushrooms, cremini)
  • Shallots or onions
  • Thyme
  • Prosciutto or Parma ham
  • Puff pastry
  • Dijon mustard
  • Egg yolks (for egg wash)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Beef:

    Sear the beef tenderloin in a hot pan with olive oil until browned on all sides. Let the beef cool, then brush it with Dijon mustard. wrap the beef tightly in plastic wrap and chill it.

  2. Make the Duxelles:

    Finely chop the mushrooms and shallots. Saut√© them with thyme until all the moisture has evaporated, creating a paste. Let the duxelles cool.

  3. Assemble the Wellington:

    Lay out prosciutto or Parma ham on a sheet of cling film. Spread the duxelles evenly over the ham. Place the seared beef on top of the duxelles. Roll the ham and duxelles tightly around the beef, using the cling film to create a log shape. Chill the beef log. Roll out the puff pastry, unwrap the beef log, and place it on the puff pastry. Wrap the puff pastry around the beef, sealing the edges. Brush the pastry with an egg wash. Score the top of the pastry.

  4. Bake:

    Preheat the oven. Bake the Wellington until the pastry is golden brown and the beef reaches your desired internal temperature. Let the Wellington rest before slicing.

Important Tips:

  • Use a high-quality beef tenderloin for the best results.
  • Ensure the duxelles are dry to prevent the pastry from becoming soggy.
  • Chill the beef and the assembled Wellington before baking to help the pastry hold its shape.
  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure the beef is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
